### `~ $ cat what-i-build.md`

> I care more about **how** something works than **that** it works. These are the corners I keep coming back to:

- 🧠 &nbsp;**AI & LLM systems** — RAG pipelines, and agents that drive browsers, fill forms, and read the screen to get things done.
- 🎯 &nbsp;**Teaching machines through reward** — reinforcement learning from Gym classics and ROS2 robots to pushing tiny 0.5B models further than they have any right to go (GRPO / LoRA).
- 🗄️ &nbsp;**Systems from scratch** — storage engines, WAL & crash recovery, B+trees, LSM-trees, and low-level observability down at the syscall layer.
- ⚙️ &nbsp;**Backend & design** — clean APIs, low-level & high-level design, and the building blocks of distributed systems (caches, rate limiters, typeahead search).
- 🌐 &nbsp;**Full-stack & apps** — React on the web, Flutter on the phone, bolted onto the things above.
- 🖥️ &nbsp;**Running my own metal** — self-hosted Linux boxes with Caddy, mail servers and DKIM, because owning the whole stack is half the fun.
